Privacy Policy for okCV

Last updated: February 24, 2026

okCV is a Free app built by Mohammad Salman. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

1. Data Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data on our servers. All information you enter (such as name, contact details, and experience) is stored locally on your device.

2. AI Features (Google Gemini API)

Our app uses the Google Gemini API to help improve your resume content. When you use AI features, only the text you provide is sent to Google's servers for processing. We do not store this data.

3. Permissions

To ensure optimal functionality while strictly safeguarding your privacy, okCV requests the following device access: • Profile Picture (Media Access): Requested strictly to allow you to select and upload a professional photo for your CV. We do not scan, index, or access your device's media gallery beyond the specific image you choose. • Storage Access: Utilized exclusively for saving the finalized PDF resume directly to your device's local storage. • Notifications: Utilized solely for local system alerts to notify you upon the successful export and download of your resume. We do not deploy push notifications for marketing or promotional purposes.
